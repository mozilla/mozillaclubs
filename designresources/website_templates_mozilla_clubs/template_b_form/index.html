We are the mozilla Cagliari Club!

Our goals is to create regular laboratories and free events to aim the people to increase their digital and web skills at all the interaction levels. Today, is extremely important that all people have the opportunity to acquire more awareness about the Internet technologies.

For this goal, Mozilla, the proudly non-profit champions of the Internet, helps to keep it healthy, open and accessible to all. In local, we of the Mozilla Cagliari Club have decided to make some free activities to aim the people to learn how read, write, participate on the web.
